Nodoguro Omakase

Nodoguro is a Japanese restaurant in Portland that offers a 25-course kaiseki-style tasting menu, blending traditional Japanese techniques with seasonal Pacific Northwest ingredients. The meal progresses from light vegetables and raw seafood to broths, steamed and grilled dishes, and ends with rice and sushi.

This was such a wonderful experience. It was my first Japanese omakase experience that wasn't just sushi. All of the dishes were amazing and the sake pairing was lovely.
